###Addition operator (+)###
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
Add two numeric values:
$a = 10
$b = 10
$c = $a + $b
$c -> 20

Add two strings:
$x = "power"
$y = "Shell"
$z = $x + $y
$z -> powerShell

Add two arrays:
$x = 1,2,3
$y = "a","b","c"
$z = $x + $y
$z
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
###Subtraction operator (-)###

Subtract one number from other value:
$a = 20
$b = 10
$c = $a - $b
$c -> 10

Make a negative number
$x = 10
$y = -$x
$y -> -10
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
###Multiplication operator (*)###

Multiplies two numeric values:
$a = 20
$b = 10
$c = $a * $b
$c -> 200

Create copy of the string:
$x = "Shell"
$y = $x*2
$y -> ShellShell

Create copy of an array:
$x = 1,2,3
$y = $x*2
$y -> 123123
//////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
###Division operator (/)###

Divide two numeric values:
$a = 25
$b = 5
$c = $a/$b
%c -> 5
/////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
###Modulus operator (%)###

$a = 10
$b = 4
$c = $a % $b
$c -> 2
